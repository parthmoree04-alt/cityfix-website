Features

Everything you need to fix your neighbourhood

Simple tools for citizens to speak up and see results.

📍

Instant Reporting

Snap a photo, confirm your GPS location and submit an issue in under 30 seconds. No forms, no friction.

👥

Community Upvoting

Neighbours can upvote your report. Issues with 10+ votes are automatically fast-tracked to the concerned authority.

🔔

Live Status Updates

Get notified at every step — when your report is received, assigned, in progress and finally resolved.

🗺️

Neighbourhood Map

See all open and resolved issues on a live map around you. Know what's being fixed in your area.

📊

Ward Dashboard

A public dashboard shows how many issues are open, in progress and resolved — keeping officials accountable.

💬

WhatsApp Integration

No app download needed. Report issues directly through a WhatsApp chatbot — using behaviour you already have.

About

About CityFix

CityFix is a Design Thinking & Innovation project that addresses the broken communication between urban citizens and municipal authorities. Built using Stanford's d.school 5-phase methodology, it turns a real urban frustration into a simple, human-centred solution.
